Forgot Password?

Password recovery is handled by the system administrator.

How Password Recovery Works

If you have forgotten your password, please contact your system administrator or HR department.

The administrator will:

  • Verify your identity
  • Reset your account password
  • Provide a temporary password
  • Require you to create a new password during your next login
🔒 For security reasons, users cannot reset passwords directly.
